What makes it different

Everything you need to dominate

A complete football sandbox — from the player creator to the final whistle.

Build Your Player

The deepest creator in Roblox football. Tune height, weight and wingspan, pick an archetype, and spend attribute points to craft a one-of-one athlete.

Authentic Gameplay

Real ball physics, multiple QB throw types, contested catches and a momentum-driven hit-stick tackling system that rewards timing and skill.

Iconic Stadiums

Take the field across detailed college venues and practice facilities, each loaded on its own server so every game has room to breathe.

Takeover & Momentum

Catch fire and earn position-specific X-Factor abilities. Break ankles, trigger badges, and swing the game with a 2K-style takeover meter.

True College Rules

A full automated officiating system calls the game like the real thing — college catch rules, overtime from the 25, penalties and live clock management.

Save & Customize

Multiple build slots let you save your athletes, swap gear and equipment, and jump straight back into your favorite setup whenever you play.
